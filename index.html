Cambridge KET Interactive Worksheet

Practice your English skills with these exercises.

Part 1: Reading Comprehension

Instructions:

Read the text about three teenagers and answer the questions below.

Ellie

I'm 12 years old and live in London with my parents, my two sisters Mel and Sue, and my little brother, Mark. Oh, and our dog, Rufus! I'm the youngest girl in my family. My best friend is called Stef. She's really funny and very good at playing the guitar. She's in a band.

Laura

I live with my mum and dad in a small town in the north. I'm nearly 13. I don't have any brothers or sisters. My mum is a musician, and my dad teaches at a university. I have to study more when I'm older. I'm having a party. My best friend is from Poland.

Chloe

I'm 13 years old - two years older than my little sister, Monica. I don't have any brothers. We live in a big family home in a village. I don't like sport, but I read a lot. My dad is a mechanic. My mum isn't from England. She teaches languages at a school.

1. Who has a brother and sisters?

2. Who doesn't like sport?

3. Who is the oldest?

4. Who has a pet?

Part 2: Grammar - Present Simple

Instructions:

Complete the sentences with the correct present simple form of the verb in brackets. Use short forms where possible (e.g., "isn't").

My best friend's name (1) (be) David. He (2) (not be) from England - he's from America. He (3) (love) sport, especially football. I (4) (not like) sport very much, but David and I (5) (be) in the same class.

Part 4: Writing

Instructions:
